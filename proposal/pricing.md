# Pricing & Commercial Terms

## Fee Structure

We propose a fixed-fee engagement rather than a time-and-materials arrangement. Fixed-fee pricing gives Meridian full cost visibility from day one: no surprises as the work unfolds, no invoice disputes at close. Given the scope of the required deliverables and the clarity of the RFP requirements, we are confident in our estimates and are willing to absorb scheduling risk on your behalf.

## Fee Breakdown

| Item | Scope | Fixed Fee |
|------|-------|-----------|
| R4 Architecture documentation | 2 days | €2,500 |
| R1 Reports module remediation | 8 days | €10,000 |
| R3 Automated browser testing | 5 days | €6,250 |
| R2 Restocking recommendations | 10 days | €12,500 |
| Project management & delivery | 5 days | €6,250 |
| **Required total (R1–R4)** | **30 days** | **€37,500** |
| D1 UI modernization *(optional)* | 5 days | €6,250 |
| D2 Internationalization *(optional)* | 4 days | €5,000 |
| D3 Dark mode *(optional)* | 3 days | €3,750 |

The desired items (D1–D3) are priced as independent optional line items. Meridian may include any combination at contract signing — or defer them entirely. There is no dependency between optional items; each can stand alone.

## What's Not Included

The fixed fees above cover all deliverables described in §4 of the RFP. The following items fall outside the engagement scope and are not priced herein:

- **Infrastructure provisioning, hosting, or cloud setup.** We deliver working software; standing up servers, configuring cloud accounts, or purchasing compute is outside scope.
- **Third-party licenses or software procurement.** Any commercial libraries, SaaS tools, or paid APIs required by Meridian's environment are procured and paid for separately by Meridian.
- **Data migration or database setup.** The current system uses JSON flat files; migrating legacy data to any new storage system, or provisioning a production database, is excluded.
- **End-user training or change management.** We will provide written documentation for each delivered feature; formal training sessions, workshops, or organizational change management are not included.
- **Design assets, brand guidelines, or custom iconography beyond existing design tokens.** UI work builds on the design tokens already present in the codebase; commissioning new brand assets or custom icon sets is outside scope.

Exclusions can be added as priced change orders at any point during the engagement.

## Not-to-Exceed Cap

Should Meridian elect to include all required and desired deliverables (R1–R4 plus D1–D3), the total engagement fee will not exceed **€52,500**. This cap is contractually binding; any scope overrun is our risk, not yours.

## Payment Terms

| Milestone | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| Contract signing | 30% (€11,250 on required scope) |
| Phase 2 completion | 40% (€15,000 on required scope) |
| Final delivery & acceptance | 30% (€11,250 on required scope) |

Optional items are invoiced at the same 30/40/30 split applied to the optional fee total. All fees are denominated in euros; adesso can provide a USD equivalent on request based on the exchange rate at contract signing, and the fixed-fee structure eliminates ongoing FX exposure for Meridian. Invoices are payable within 30 days of issuance.
